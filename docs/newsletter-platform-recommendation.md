# Newsletter Platform Recommendation for MitLivMed

**Date:** January 2026
**Purpose:** Evaluate email marketing platforms for waitlist newsletter

---

## Executive Summary

**Recommendation: Brevo** is the best choice for MitLivMed's simple newsletter needs. It offers a more generous free tier, is designed for general business use (not ecommerce-specific), and integrates with Tally.so via Zapier.

---

## Platform Comparison

| Factor | Brevo | Omnisend |
|--------|-------|----------|
| **Free tier emails** | 300/day (~9,000/month) | 500/month |
| **Free tier contacts** | Unlimited | 250 |
| **Platform focus** | General business | Ecommerce |
| **Branding on free** | No watermark | Watermark on emails |
| **Tally.so integration** | Via Zapier | Via Zapier |
| **Support** | Email support | 24/7 live chat |

---

## Why Brevo Wins

### 1. Much More Generous Free Tier
- **18x more emails** per month (9,000 vs 500)
- Unlimited contacts vs 250 contact limit
- No watermark/branding on emails

### 2. Better Fit for Non-Ecommerce
- Omnisend is built for online stores with features like cart abandonment, product recommendations, and Shopify integration
- Brevo is designed for general newsletters and business communication
- Cleaner interface without unnecessary ecommerce tools

### 3. Same Integration Capabilities
- Both platforms connect to Tally.so through Zapier
- No advantage either way for the integration setup

---

## Recommended Integration: Tally → Zapier → Brevo

### Why Direct Zapier Integration (not via Google Sheets)

| Approach | Pros | Cons |
|----------|------|------|
| **Tally → Zapier → Brevo** | Simple, real-time, one integration | Requires Zapier account |
| **Tally → Sheets → Brevo** | Spreadsheet backup | Two integrations, hourly sync delay |

**Recommendation:** Use the direct Zapier integration for simplicity and real-time contact addition.

### Zapier Free Tier
- 100 tasks/month (sufficient for early-stage signups)
- Upgrade available as volume grows

---

## Setup Steps

1. **Create Brevo account** at brevo.com (free)
2. **Create Zapier account** at zapier.com (free)
3. **Create a Zap:**
   - Trigger: "New Tally.so submission"
   - Action: "Create Brevo contact"
   - Map email field from Tally to Brevo
4. **Test the integration** with a test form submission
