# Signup Form PRD

## Purpose
Design the waitlist signup form for MitLivMed's pre-launch landing page. The form should capture email signups while preemptively answering logistical questions users have.

---

## Context

**Product:** MitLivMed — a moderated digital community for people with bipolar disorder
**Page:** Pre-launch landing page (Danish)
**Goal:** Build a waitlist for early access launching early 2026

The form appears after the "What is / What isn't" sections. Users at this point understand the product — now they need confidence about what happens next.

---

## Logistical Questions to Address

These questions were previously in a FAQ. They should now be answered within or around the signup form:

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Hvornår åbner det? | Tidlig adgang åbner i starten af 2026 |
| Hvordan får jeg tidlig adgang? | Skriv dig op, så får du besked først |
| Hvad sker der, efter jeg har skrevet mig op? | Du får lejlighedsvise opdateringer (ingen spam). Når vi lancerer, er du først i køen. |

---

## Requirements

### Must have
- Email input field
- Clear CTA button
- Answers the 3 logistical questions (inline, microcopy, or progressive disclosure)
- Mobile-friendly
- Success state with confirmation message

### Should have
- Visual indication of "no spam" promise
- Mention of "early 2026" timing near CTA
- Warm, conversational tone matching the rest of the page

### Could have
- Optional name field
- Option to indicate: "Jeg er pårørende" (I'm a supporter/family member)
- Social proof (e.g., "Join X others on the waitlist")

---

## Copy Elements

**CTA button:** "Skriv dig op til tidlig adgang"

**Supporting microcopy options:**
- "MitLivMed åbner i starten af 2026"
- "Du får besked først — ingen spam"
- "Lejlighedsvise opdateringer, og først i køen ved lancering"

**Success message:**
- "Tak! Du er skrevet op. Vi sender dig besked, når MitLivMed åbner."

---

## Design Constraints

- Must work on mobile (primary use case)
- Keep form minimal — don't ask for more than necessary
- Should feel safe and low-commitment
- Align with warm, supportive brand tone

---

## Open for Exploration

- Form layout (inline vs. card vs. modal)
- How to surface the 3 logistical answers (always visible vs. expandable vs. post-submit)
- Whether to add optional fields (name, pårørende checkbox)
- Visual treatment of the "no spam" promise
